Field Guide

EcoRift links each mission action to real environmental outcomes. Use this guide when you hit Esc.

Game Outline

1) Scan and identify ecosystem stress in each region.

2) Neutralize active pollution emitters to stop damage at the source.

3) Run restoration actions (cleanup, filter, replant, stabilize, energy transition).

4) Resolve disasters quickly before they trigger climate penalties.

5) Keep healthy metrics above unhealthy metrics for long-term recovery.

Real-World Why

Air pollution: PM2.5 and NOx increase asthma and heart risks.

Water contamination: toxins and plastic reduce oxygen and collapse food webs.

Biodiversity loss: ecosystems lose resilience to heat, floods, and disease.

Dirty energy: raises CO2 and storm severity over time.

Support the Environment

Choose low-waste products and reduce single-use plastics.

Use public transit, bike, or efficient vehicles when possible.

Support local restoration groups and habitat protection efforts.

Advocate for clean energy and responsible industrial policies.
